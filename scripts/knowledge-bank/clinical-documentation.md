# Clinical Documentation Knowledge Bank

## SOAP Note Structure (AU Standard)

### Subjective
- PC (Presenting Complaint): Chief concern in patient's words
- HPC: SOCRATES for pain (Site, Onset, Character, Radiation, Associations, Timing, Exacerbating/relieving, Severity)
- PMHx: Active conditions with dates, surgical history
- DHx: Current medications (name, dose, frequency, indication)
- Allergies: Drug name + nature of reaction
- FHx: First-degree relatives, conditions, age of onset
- SHx: Smoking (pack-years), alcohol (std drinks/week), occupation, living situation, exercise, diet

### Objective
- Vitals: HR, BP, RR, SpO2, Temp, Weight, Height, BMI, Waist circumference
- General appearance
- Examination findings (relevant systems)
- Investigation results

### Assessment
1. Primary diagnosis (ICD-10-AM code)
2. Secondary diagnoses
3. Differential diagnoses
4. Problem list (active + chronic)

### Plan
- Investigations ordered
- Medications (new/changed/ceased with rationale)
- Non-pharmacological (lifestyle, allied health referrals)
- Referrals (specialty, urgency, clinical question)
- Follow-up timing
- Safety netting (red flags, when to seek help)
- Patient understanding confirmed

## Referral Letter Template (AU)

Key elements:
- Practice letterhead, date
- Specialist name and specialty
- Patient: full name, DOB, Medicare number, address, phone
- "Thank you for seeing [title] [surname] regarding [clinical question]"
- Presenting concern (2-3 sentences)
- Relevant history
- Current medications with doses
- Allergies
- Investigation results (copies enclosed)
- Examination findings
- Assessment and specific clinical question
- Urgency indication
- GP details: AHPRA number, Provider number

## Care Plan Templates

### GPMP (Item 721)
- Chronic conditions addressed (ICD-10-AM codes)
- Patient's health needs and goals (in their words)
- Medication management plan
- Lifestyle modifications (diet, exercise, smoking, alcohol, weight target)
- Investigations and monitoring schedule
- Allied health referrals
- Agreed actions: patient will / GP will / other providers will
- Review date (within 6 months)
- Signatures (patient + GP)

### TCA (Item 723)
- Minimum 3 providers from different disciplines
- Each provider: name, role, specific actions, Medicare items
- Communication plan between providers
- Allied health allocation (max 5 sessions/year total)
- Review date

### Mental Health Treatment Plan (Item 2700/2715)
- Assessment: presenting issues, history, risk assessment
- Screening tools: PHQ-9, K10, GAD-7 scores with severity
- Diagnosis (DSM-5/ICD-10)
- Pharmacotherapy plan
- Psychological therapy referral (CBT/ACT/IPT — up to 10 sessions/year)
- Lifestyle interventions
- Crisis plan: support person, Lifeline 13 11 14, local ED
- Review in 4-6 weeks

## Weight Management Initial Consultation Template

Key sections:
- Anthropometrics: weight, BMI, waist circumference
- Weight history: lifetime highest, trajectory, triggers
- Previous attempts: diets, exercise, medications, surgery
- Comorbidity screen: T2DM, HTN, OSA, PCOS, NAFLD, depression
- Edmonton Obesity Staging: Stage 0-4
- Eating behaviour screening: binge eating, emotional eating, night eating
- Mental health: PHQ-9, STOP-BANG for OSA
- Pathology: FBC, UEC, LFTs, HbA1c, fasting glucose, lipids, TFTs, iron studies
- Pharmacotherapy eligibility assessment (PBS criteria check)
- Plan: medication + lifestyle + monitoring + referrals + safety netting

## Telehealth Documentation Requirements
Additional to standard notes:
- Consultation modality (video/phone)
- Platform used
- Patient's location (state/territory)
- Informed consent for telehealth
- Identity verification method
- Clinical suitability confirmed
- Physical examination limitations noted
- In-person follow-up needed (yes/no, timeframe)

## Australian English Clinical Conventions
- Behaviour, colour, organisation, specialised, recognised
- Haemoglobin, oestrogen, oedema, faeces, anaesthesia, paediatric
- Appendicectomy (not appendectomy)
- Adrenaline (not epinephrine)
- Frusemide (not furosemide)
- Salbutamol (not albuterol)
- Paracetamol (not acetaminophen)
- Date: DD/MM/YYYY
- Metric: kg, cm, mmol/L, degrees C
