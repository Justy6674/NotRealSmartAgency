# NP Endorsement Knowledge Bank (EndorseMe)

## Core Requirements
1. Clean, unrestricted RN registration (no conditions/undertakings)
2. 5,000 hours advanced practice in past 6 years
3. NMBA-approved Master's qualification (Pathway 1) or equivalent (Pathway 2)
4. Demonstrated competence against 4 NP Standards for Practice (24 sub-criteria)
5. Professional Indemnity Insurance current

## 4 NP Standards for Practice (2021)

### Standard 1: Assesses Using Diagnostic Capability (6 sub-criteria)
1.1 Advanced health assessment (history, exam, investigations)
1.2 Synthesises complex/conflicting data from multiple sources
1.3 Formulates differential diagnoses using clinical reasoning
1.4 Orders, performs, interprets diagnostic tests within scope
1.5 Recognises deterioration, initiates timely intervention
1.6 Critically reflects on assessment decisions

### Standard 2: Plans Care and Engages Others (6 sub-criteria)
2.1 Person-centred, evidence-informed care plans
2.2 Considers quality of life, preferences, culture, social determinants
2.3 Collaborates with other health professionals
2.4 Coordinates care across settings and transitions
2.5 Clinical leadership within interprofessional teams
2.6 Advocates for person and system improvements

### Standard 3: Prescribes and Implements Therapeutic Interventions (6 sub-criteria)
3.1 Prescribes safely consistent with legislation
3.2 Monitors response, adjusts plans
3.3 Provides education about medicines and self-management
3.4 Non-pharmacological and culturally safe interventions
3.5 Manages adverse effects and complications
3.6 Documents clearly, accurately, timely

### Standard 4: Supports Health Systems (6 sub-criteria)
4.1 Clinical governance, QI, risk management
4.2 Uses data/audit to evaluate practice
4.3 Education, mentoring, development of others
4.4 Research and evidence to inform practice
4.5 Policy, service planning, advocacy
4.6 Professional development and supervision

## Portfolio Requirements (7 Appendices)
1. Education documentation (certified degree + transcript)
2. Signed, dated CV with Standards mapping
3. Minimum 2 letters of support (within 3 months, on letterhead)
4. Statements of service from all employers
5. CPD evidence (30 hours/year: 20 general + 10 NP-specific)
6. Case exemplars (min 2), leadership, research, education evidence
7. Competency mapping (Pathway 2 only)

## 5,000 Hours Critical Rules
- Must be within 6 years before application date
- Master's practicum hours do NOT count
- Must be advanced practice level (not standard RN duties)
- Can span multiple employers
- Each employer needs a Statement of Service

## Post-Endorsement Requirements
- Annual renewal by 31 May
- 450 hours clinical practice in past 5 years (recency)
- 30 hours CPD annually (20 general + 10 NP-specific)
- PII maintained continuously
- Random audit: 4 weeks to provide documentation
