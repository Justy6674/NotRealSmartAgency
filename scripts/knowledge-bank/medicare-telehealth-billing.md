## PBS Authority for GLP-1 Medications

### Ozempic (T2DM)
- PBS Items: 12622B (0.25/0.5mg pen), 12623C (1mg pen)
- Authority Required (phone 1800 888 333 or PBS Online)
- Criteria: T2DM, HbA1c >7%, BMI >35, with metformin, not with insulin
- Initial: 6 months supply
- Continuing: HbA1c reduction ≥0.5% from baseline

### Wegovy (Obesity)
- Authority Required (Streamlined)
- Criteria: BMI ≥30, or ≥27 with comorbidity
- With lifestyle intervention (diet + exercise)
- Not with other GLP-1 RAs

## Telehealth Compliance — 12-Month Rule

### The Rule
Medicare telehealth requires patients to have had an in-person visit with the same practice within the past 12 months.

### 9 Exemption Categories
Patients exempt from the 12-month rule:
1. Living in a Modified Monash Model (MMM) 4-7 area (regional/remote)
2. Homeless or at risk of homelessness
3. Living in residential aged care
4. Under 12 months of age
5. Receiving services relating to pregnancy
6. Receiving services under a Mental Health Treatment Plan
7. Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander peoples
8. Receiving end-of-life/palliative care
9. Disaster-declared areas (temporary exemption)
